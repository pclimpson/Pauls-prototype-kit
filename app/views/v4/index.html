To request a repair for a communal area, please see customer services contact details page.

What is a communal area?

Communal repairs are usually in areas that people share.

For example:

  • repairs to door entry systems
  • lock repairs to public doors
  • lighting repairs to shared areas
  • glazing repairs to shared doors or stairway windows
  • roof and gutter repairs
  • structure and external walls to your block
! Warning If you suspect you have a gas leak, you must report it immediately to the Gas Emergency Service on 0800 111 999 or via textphone (minicom) on 0800 371 787
! Warning For other emergency repairs, please see customer services contact details page.
